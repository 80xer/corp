In Mid Translation

Created by Tracy Featherstone
"In Mid Translation explores the way ideas are translated across visual media, cultural identity, and time.
I am specifically interested in the information that must be fabricated during translation. Although there will be a factual similarity, there are multiple decisions regarding media, spatial relationships etc.
that need to be improvised in order to make the translated work exist in its new form.
Therefore something that begins as a type of academic or literal translation must default to subjectivity on the part of the artist.
This is the precise moment that I am exploring. When fact must submit to subjectivity.
What has been morphed or skewed? Do the new dimensional qualities give the form a transformed meaning?"
