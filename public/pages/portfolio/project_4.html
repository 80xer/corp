Excretions

Created by Jazoo Yang

In the process of creating large-scale excretions, Jazoo structures her own body as a tool of expression. The use of the hands to apply paint, andthe momentum and intensity of her moving body, govern the form of her work. As if unconscious, Jazoo focuses her solitary energy (氣, qì) on a given area, allowing the action of painting to be documented by the resulting strokes and scratches. The excretions, then, are a form of evidential catharsis, available for shared contemplation by artist and viewer. Jazoo’s highly physical method results in works that are grand in scale, yet also intimate and expressive. She intends on providing for the viewer a glimpse into her soul, a genuine expression of her own solitude and a gesture of humanity within the bleakness of the big city.
