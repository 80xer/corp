MIAMI HOUSES

"Referencing the work of the Becher, of Düsseldorf school of visual art of the 70's.
Through repetition of a strict formal composition, the initial understanding of the function of the subject gradually fades as an analysis of the form of the subject.
The repetition creates an inevitable comparison between images, thus informing the viewer as to the multiplicity of differences.."
