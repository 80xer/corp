What's Inside

THISGROUND is a social network service that allows users to share their creative activities.

UPLOAD

Users can easily upload their work through the import and export features.

SHARE

All contents can be shared
regardless of their genre.

NETWORK

Artists all over the world can communicate with one another in one space.
